Recipe for "THE UNEXPECTED" relationship

Ingredients:

  • Three weeks of study abroad
  • 3 years of long distance relationship
  • A unique taxi driver for your trips in Rome
  • 8 trips across the Atlantic Ocean
  • Lots of family moments
  • Optional: Add a family trip to Disney World (that depends on whether you want for the relationship to last)

Directions:

Take your pot and start adding all the ingredients. Now patience is key, you want for each ingredient's taste to be preserved and not just blend with each other; that way everything will take a part in making the whole thing better without taking the uniqueness from each individual element. In order to achieve that, add them slowly. When you feel that it has reached the right amount of good moments, lessons and growth together, remove from heat
and start enjoying it!
